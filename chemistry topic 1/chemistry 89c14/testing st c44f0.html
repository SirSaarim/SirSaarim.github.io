  • what is the greenhouse effect?
    1. electromagnetic solar radiation in the form of visible light and UV radiation travels toward the earth
    1. atoms and molecules absorb energy in the atmosphere and surface of earth
    1. short wave radiation is re-radiated off earth's surface / atmosphere as long-wave radiation from the heated molecules in the earth's atmosphere and surface
    1. some infrared radiation leaves the earth's atmosphere and enters the interplanetary space
    1. greenhouse gases in the atmosphere absorb the rest of the radiation and re-radiate it in a random direction due to their bonding structure
    1. this traps heat closer to the earth's surface, which establishes a thermal balance and therefore a stable temperature
